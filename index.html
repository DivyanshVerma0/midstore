Fyodor Dostoevsky

Bibliophile

Fyodor Dostoevsky, a Russian novelist, is renowned for his profound explorations of human psychology, morality, and existentialism. His works, like Crime and Punishment and The Brothers Karamazov, delve into complex themes such as guilt, redemption, free will, and the struggle between good and evil.
